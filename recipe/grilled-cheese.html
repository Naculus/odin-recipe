Description

Toast bread with melty cheese

Cruchity, ooey, gooey, goodness!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Butter the outside of the bread
  2. Add salt & pepper
  3. Place bread in warm skillet butter side down
  4. Place cheese slice on both side
  5. When bread is toasted, close and enjoy

Flappy's best fluffy pancakes

Flappy uses these ingredients to make the pancakes fluffy

Ingredients

  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredients
  2. Pour in buttermilk
  3. Mix
  4. Heat skillet
  5. Add butter
  6. Cook until bubbles pop on top and the bottom has a golden brown color
